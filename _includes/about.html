Scire licet: Latin for "it is permitted to know". I love learning!

Before falling in love with eduation, I studied art and history while analyzing numbers for an international chain brand. Once AmeriCorps showed me education's potential for empowerment and social justice, I was hooked. Now I'm learning all I can about programming and technology in order to use those skills to broaden access to all knowledge for everyone.

In my spare time, I enjoy:

Language Learning

While I'm fluent in Spanish, I am also proficient in Portuguese. I'm currently studying German, and I hope to learn even more languages in the future.

Public Service

I served three years in AmeriCorps, where I worked to increase childhood & adult literacy in at-risk communities.

Philosophy

I'm the secretary of a philosophy reading group where we read Hegel and others who have been influenced by him.

Film

I love a good sci-fi, drama, action, comedy, or classic movie.
